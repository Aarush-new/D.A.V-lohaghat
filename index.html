About Us

Welcome to D.A.V. Lohaghat School, a premier educational institution nestled in the breathtaking landscapes of Uttarkhand, India. We are proud to offer a comprehensive curriculum that spans from nursery to class 12, providing our students with a solid foundation for academic and personal growth.

At D.A.V. Lohaghat School, we are committed to creating a nurturing environment where students can flourish both academically and socially. Our experienced faculty members are dedicated to fostering a culture of learning and exploration, empowering students to discover their passions and pursue their dreams.

With a focus on holistic education, we aim to develop well-rounded individuals who are not just academically proficient, but also socially conscious and responsible citizens of society. Through our innovative teaching methods, state-of-the-art facilities, and a wide range of extracurricular activities, we strive to instill in our students a lifelong love for learning and a spirit of curiosity.

Join us on this exciting journey of discovery and growth, as we work together to shape the future leaders of tomorrow.
